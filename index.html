Features

More to Discover

We waited until we could do it right. Then we did! Instead of creating a carbon copy.

Dashboard

Dashboard is first page you will see once you login. In Dashboard you can see current server resource usage in form of pie charts, take a quick look at your apps and their status, manage TODOs so you can stay organized.

My Apps pages

Manage Apps allows you to see list of all your Apps, something like quick look on dashboard, but this includes more features like NPM Install of dependencies, Start/Stop, Modify and more to come.

Add App allows you to quickly add your new app, our App Deployer wizzard will do everything for you, you just need to know your app name, entry point and port.

Account settings

This is boring, but helpful part of our platform. Here you can change your current account password (this will change SFTP login as well), see connection info, URLs for your Apps, Wipe TODOs and delete your account (this will remove your account from our platform and delete all your apps. Use with caution).
